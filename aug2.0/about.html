Our Story

Founded in 2009 in Abidjan, Ivory Coast, Africure has grown from a small pharmaceutical startup to one of West Africa's most trusted healthcare companies. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality healthcare accessible and affordable for every African family.

Today, we operate across 20+ African countries, manufacturing and distributing over 50 essential medicines that serve millions of people. Our commitment to excellence has earned us WHO-GMP certification and recognition as a leader in pharmaceutical innovation.

Our Mission

To improve the health and well-being of African communities by providing affordable, high-quality pharmaceutical products and healthcare solutions that meet international standards.

Our Vision

To be the leading pharmaceutical company in Africa, recognized for our innovation, quality, and commitment to making healthcare accessible to all, while contributing to the continent's economic development.

Our Values

  • Quality: Uncompromising commitment to excellence
  • Integrity: Ethical practices in all we do
  • Innovation: Continuous improvement and advancement
  • Accessibility: Making healthcare affordable for all
  • Community: Supporting African development
