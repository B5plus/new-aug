Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I become a distributor for Africure products?

We welcome partnership inquiries from qualified distributors. Please contact our sales team at sales@africure.com with your company information, distribution experience, and target markets.

What certifications do your products have?

All Africure products are manufactured in WHO-GMP certified facilities and comply with international pharmaceutical standards. Many of our products are WHO prequalified.

Do you offer product samples for evaluation?

Yes, we provide product samples to qualified healthcare professionals and potential partners. Please contact us with your credentials and specific product interests.

How can I report an adverse event or product quality issue?

Patient safety is our priority. Please report any adverse events or quality concerns immediately to our pharmacovigilance team at safety@africure.com or call our emergency hotline.
